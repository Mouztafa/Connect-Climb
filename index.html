Onboarding Strategy
NTI | DEY Initiative
Class of 2025

The Strategic Vision

Bridging the gap between technical potential and professional performance.

The Problem

High Turnover. Junior engineers feel isolated and overwhelmed. They lack the "soft" infrastructure to survive corporate culture, leading to early burnout.

The Fix

Structured Integration. Using Organizational Behavior (OB) principles to define culture and creating clear integration pathways from Day 1.

The Tool

Performance Tracking. Measuring "intangibles" like Emotional Intelligence (EQ), Adaptability, and Ethics alongside technical KPIs.

The Model

Head, Heart, Hands. A holistic mentorship approach: Growth Mindset (Head), Connection (Heart), and Technical Execution (Hands).

2-Week Onboarding Roadmap

A structured daily agenda to ensure successful integration.

Week 1: Foundation

Culture & EQ
Day 1: Structure Culture
  • OB Concept: Mechanistic vs. Organic Structures.
  • Action: Map reporting lines.
Day 2: Ethics & Brand Pro
  • Ethics: Integrity when unseen.
  • Action: LinkedIn Audit.
Day 3: Communication Meetings
  • Rules: Agenda, Time, Minutes.
  • Action: Draft Minutes.
Days 4-5: EQ Workshop Soft Skills
  • Focus: Self-Awareness & Empathy.
  • Action: Team Bonding Activity.

Week 2: Execution

Strategy & Action
Day 6: Adaptability Agile
  • Simulation: "Shifting Priorities" crisis.
  • Focus: Proactive Response.
Day 7: Productivity Time
  • Tool: Eisenhower Matrix.
  • Stress: Remove/Change/Accept.
Day 8: Critical Thinking Logic
  • Tool: Bloom's Taxonomy.
  • Focus: Identifying Fallacies.
Days 9-10: Graduation Goal
  • Mentorship: Head, Heart, Hands.
  • Final: 90-Day SMART Plan.
